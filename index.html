FAQs

Wherever you need us, we're always here for you. We love to have one-on-one conversations with our community and are always open to answer your questions.

01 What is the purpose of this app?

Its aim is to establish a loud and proud online home for your favorite women's football club. A place where fans are the main residents, actively supporting their club while also enjoying the experience.

02Which clubs can get this app?

Any women's football club within the EU, registered with their national football association, is eligible to get the app.

03Can I really get the app for my club?

Absolutely! You can kickstart the process by voting, sharing, and promoting the idea. We’ll handle the rest, from gathering votes to contacting your club and developing the app.

04When will my club get the app after I vote?

As soon as we receive interest from 50 fans of your club, we will contact your club immediately. Once the club confirms, the setup process will take less than a month.

05Who pays for the app, clubs or fans?

The app is completely free for both clubs and fans. Our goal is to financially support clubs by increasing visibility and attracting more partners and sponsors.

06How can the app help my club?

The fans are the driving force of any club, a community capable of turning any situation around for the best with the right tools. This app provides just that - a fancy online space equipped with all the tools you need.
